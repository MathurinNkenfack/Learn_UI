PREMIER SERVICE

Your exclusive concierge-style service

Our Premier Service is a high end currency transaction service for private high net worth clients. Whether buying a villa in St Tropez, importing a work of fine art, hiring a private jet or yacht abroad or paying boarding school fees, The Premier Service can take care of all your foreign currency exchange needs.Your Premier Account Manager will be on hand to provide a concierge-style service, with expert knowledge and the highest standards of service. Everything will be explained in full detail, step by step.

FCA authorised

We will tailor your account to your specific FX requirements and help you avoid market fluctuations. Acting as your eyes on the market, we will ensure you always get the best currency exchange rate.
