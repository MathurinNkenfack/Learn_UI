BECOME AN AFFILIATE

Add value to your business the easy way

Do your private or business clients need foreign exchange with competitive rates? And always with a smooth and attentive service? Whether you’re an estate agent, property developer, lawyer, financial advisor, migration consultant, or an agent for international trade, our free affiliation service will add value to your business.

Dedicated Relationship Manager

Your Relationship Manager will work with you to integrate our services into your company’s customer journey and make the referral process simple and easy. We’ll always send you a client payment confirmation to keep you in control and make your work more efficient.
